Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for its mountains, adventure sports, and peaceful natural beauty.

Top 5 Attractions in Manali

  1. Solang Valley – Adventure sports hub
  2. Rohtang Pass – Snow-covered scenic mountain pass
  3. Hadimba Temple – Historic temple in cedar forest
  4. Old Manali – Popular for cafés and relaxed atmosphere
  5. Jogini Waterfalls – Beautiful trekking destination

Must-Try Foods in Manali

  • Siddu – Traditional steamed bread
  • Thukpa – Tibetan noodle soup
  • Trout Fish – Local fish specialty
  • Babru – Stuffed fried bread
  • Mittha – Sweet rice dessert
