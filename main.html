Favorite Holiday Destination

Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, and flowing rivers, it is one of the most popular holiday destinations for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports like paragliding and skiing.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for breathtaking snowy landscapes.
  3. Hadimba Temple – A historic temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for cafes, culture, and scenic beauty.
  5. Beas River – Ideal for rafting and riverside relaxation.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – Traditional Himachali steamed bread.
  • Thukpa – Hot Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Trout Fish – Fresh local fish delicacy.
  • Babru – Himachali-style stuffed kachori.
  • Mittha – Sweet rice dessert of Himachal.
